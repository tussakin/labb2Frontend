Organized chaos!

It might sound like a bad thing, to be called chaotic, but I have turned it to my advantage.

I've always been a creative person, someone who's not afraid to get my hands dirty if it means a project get done or I get to try something new. I've tried everything from painting to clay, foam-models and 3d-modelling. Any new skill is a good skill and I am up for the challenge.
As a person, being chaotic made me creative and has given me a different way of thinking. I see patterns and solutions others often can't. I see the world differently.
As a designer, it made me think outside the box and it gave me edge. My chaotic side gives my creations a signature. A signature that not only makes my work uniqe, but also gives them life.
And most important, as a programmer I am a problem solver all the way to my core. The combination of problem solving and organized chaos makes me creative in my coding style, it makes my code a good combination of structured and inventive. I love finding new ways to solve problems and that is what makes programming my passion and future. I am constantly developing, pushing my own limits further and the possibilites are endless.
